GitCocktail: GitHub Repository Scraper and Cocktail Identity Card Generator

About this project

GitCocktail was developed by João Loureiro as part of a project for a dissertation for the Master’s in Informatics Engineering at Universidade do Minho in Braga, Portugal, under the guidance of Pedro Rangel Henriques and Alvaro Costa Neto.

It is integrated as part of a larger research project on Programing Cocktails (an agglomerate of computer programming technologies that are used to develop a specific software application), serving as a way to automate the creation of Cocktail Identity Cards (CIC). These utilize the OntoCoq ontology powered by OntoDL, and serve as a way to identify a project's Ingredients, that is, the distinct components that comprise it.
